BetaCraft is a Polish group devoted to the old-school Minecraft era. It was founded back in 2013, when we launched the first Minecraft nostalgia server. Our current projects are:

  • the old-school b1.7.3 Minecraft server (since May 2013)
  • the Betacraft Launcher, which brings back legacy versions support (since August 2018)
  • the Betacraft Proxy, which fixes inaccuracies common in other launchers (since March 2018)

The proxy and the launcher aim to fix some of the biggest flaws of the official Minecraft launcher in regard to legacy versions. For example, they provide fixes for skins, capes, lack of sound effects, client crashes, etc.

Our server is available 24/7 and its primary language is English. Players can enjoy Freebuild and Factions gameplay types.
